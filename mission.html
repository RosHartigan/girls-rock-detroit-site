Our Mission

Girls Rock Detroit is a community organization dedicated to fostering girls' creative expression, positive self-esteem, and community awareness through rock music education and performance.
By encouraging creativity, confidence, and collaboration in a supportive environment, we strive to empower young women to challenge preconceived notions of what they can do and what they can become, and to become engaged members of our communities.
We believe it is crucial that girls have access to equipment and instruction regardless of their socio-economic background, race or ethnicity.
