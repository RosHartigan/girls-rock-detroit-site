Who We Are

GRD was formed in September of 2014 by a group of women from metro Detroit. We are an LGBTQ friendly organization and strive to provide a safe, visible atmosphere for all.

We are currently pursuing membership in the national Girls Rock Camp alliance, and are seeking 501(c) status.
